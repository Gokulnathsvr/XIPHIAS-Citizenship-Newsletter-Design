The Evolving Landscape of Global Mobility

Dear Valued Client,

As we navigate through 2026, the strategic importance of holding a second citizenship has never been more pronounced. Whether your goal is to secure a safe haven for your family, unlock borderless business opportunities, or achieve optimal tax structuring, Citizenship by Investment (CBI) programs remain the definitive solution.

Recently, we have witnessed a paradigm shift. Caribbean nations have implemented standardized pricing and enhanced due diligence, elevating the prestige of their passports. Meanwhile, strategic hubs like Turkey continue to offer lucrative real estate investment routes, and emerging programs in Vanuatu provide unprecedented speed.

In this digest, we explore the top programs that are shaping the future of global mobility. Our Private Client Advisory team at XIPHIAS is here to help you select the path that perfectly aligns with your legacy.

— The XIPHIAS Private Client Advisory Team
The Platinum Standard

St. Kitts & Nevis

Established in 1984, the St. Kitts and Nevis CBI program is the oldest and most trusted in the world. It offers a fast-track route to a powerful passport with excellent global reach.

  • Visa-free travel to 155+ countries, including the EU and UK
  • No income, wealth, or inheritance taxes
  • Confidential processing within 4-6 months

The E-2 Gateway

Grenada

Grenada stands out uniquely among its Caribbean peers. It not only provides a phenomenal passport but also holds a highly coveted E-2 Investor Visa treaty with the United States.

  • Direct pathway to US Residency via the E-2 Visa
  • Visa-free access to China, Russia, and the EU
  • Zero physical residency requirements

Strategic Real Estate

Turkey (Türkiye)

Turkey offers a unique blend of European and Asian lifestyles. Its CIP allows investors to acquire citizenship by purchasing real estate in one of the world's most dynamic property markets.

  • High ROI potential on Istanbul real estate
  • Citizenship granted in just 3-5 months
  • Access to a massive transcontinental market

The Fastest Route

Vanuatu

For investors seeking speed and efficiency, the Vanuatu Development Support Program (VDSP) is unmatched, offering one of the fastest processing times globally.

  • Citizenship approval in as little as 1-2 months
  • Tax-friendly environment with no personal taxation
  • A beautiful, developing luxury tourism destination
